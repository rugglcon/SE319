Architecture design process
	* utility tree
		* a way to record ASRs all in one place
		* establishes priority of each ASR in terms of:
			* impact on architecture
			* business or mission value
	* application overview
		* create an overview of what your application will look like 
			when it is complete
		* determine what type of application you are building
			* i.e. a mobile application, a rich client
		* identity your deployment constraints
		* identify import architecture
		* decompose the functionalities of the system based on a specific
			strategy
			* based on entities: device, service, etc.
			* based on common functionalities
